Chaoqun You

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at Singapore Univertiy of Techology and Design (SUTD) working with Prof. Tony Q. S. Quek. At University of Electronic Science and Technology of China（UESTC), I earned my Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Lemin Li in Communication and Computer Science. Before that, I received my B.S. degree in Communication and Information Engineering at UESTC.
